Our Story: Building the Peninsula, One Project at a Time

Seaside Landscaping was founded right here on the Mornington Peninsula with a clear vision: to provide expert excavation, durable wall construction, and lush turfing services that truly last. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities of coastal and regional landscapes, and we're passionate about creating outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Our journey began with a commitment to quality, a deep respect for the environment, and a desire to deliver honest, reliable service. Over the years, we've grown, but our core values remain the same. We take pride in every project, big or small, and ensure that our work contributes positively to the natural beauty and resilience of the Peninsula.
