Retaining Walls

Engineered to last. Built to impress.

Durable Retaining Walls for the Peninsula Landscape

At Seaside Landscaping, we specialise in constructing high-quality retaining walls that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Whether you need to manage a sloped block, prevent soil erosion, or create more usable terraced space, our walls are built with precision and local expertise.

We ensure every wall has proper drainage, robust footings, and is constructed using materials best suited for the coastal Mornington Peninsula environment.

Our Retaining Wall Solutions

We primarily work with two types of materials, chosen for their durability and style.

Concrete Sleeper Walls

The ultimate choice for strength and longevity. Concrete sleepers are resistant to rot, termites, and the harsh coastal weather. Available in a variety of finishes and colours, they offer a clean, modern look that complements any property.

  • Maximum durability and strength
  • Low maintenance and long-lasting
  • Modern and versatile aesthetic

Treated Pine & Hardwood Walls

For a classic and natural look, timber retaining walls are an excellent choice. We use high-quality, treated pine or durable hardwoods to ensure your wall stands the test of time, blending seamlessly with your garden and landscaping.

  • Natural, rustic aesthetic
  • Cost-effective and versatile
  • Ideal for garden beds and terracing
