Building the Foundations of a Great Landscape

We specialise in landscape construction - the structural elements that provide the essential "bones" for your outdoor space. At Seaside Landscaping, we craft durable and stylish features that enhance your property's value and usability.

From stunning natural rock walls to elegant feature fencing and practical pathways, we use quality materials and expert construction techniques to ensure your investment stands the test of time on the Mornington Peninsula.

Our Landscape Construction Services

Creating functional and beautiful outdoor features that last.

Natural Rock Walls

Using locally sourced stone, we build robust and beautiful rock walls that add a timeless, natural appeal to any landscape. Perfect for creating garden tiers, retaining sloped land, or as a stunning standalone feature, our rock walls are constructed with an expert eye for detail and stability.

  • Retaining and feature walls
  • Garden tiers and terracing
  • Durable and natural aesthetic

Feature & Batten Fencing

More than just a boundary, a feature fence adds architectural style and privacy to your property. We specialise in modern batten fencing and other custom designs that act as a stylish backdrop for your garden or a standout feature for your home's facade, built with quality materials for longevity.

  • Modern privacy screens
  • Timber batten fencing
  • Custom feature designs

Steel Garden Edging

For a clean, contemporary finish, nothing beats steel edging. We install durable steel borders to define garden beds, contain pathways, and create sharp, clean lines between lawn and garden areas, preventing mulch or toppings from spilling over.

  • Defines garden beds and paths
  • Creates a sharp, modern look
  • Durable and long-lasting

Paths & Toppings

We supply and install a range of decorative and practical toppings for driveways, paths, and entertainment areas. Options like crushed rock or decorative gravel create durable, low-maintenance surfaces that complement your home and garden.

  • Driveways and crossover paths
  • Crushed rock and gravel surfaces
  • Low-maintenance and practical
