Our Services

Expert solutions for every stage of your landscape project.

Transforming Your Property, From the Ground Up

We provide a complete solution for your landscape project. Our services cover every essential step, from initial site preparation and structural work to the final installation of beautiful lawns and features. Explore our core services below to see how we build landscapes made to last.

Landscaping & Features

We specialise in the structural elements that define your outdoor space. This includes crafting beautiful natural rock walls, installing feature fencing and privacy screens, creating clean steel garden edging, and laying durable paths and driveways.

View Landscaping

Retaining Walls

We build structurally sound and visually stunning retaining walls to manage slopes, create usable garden tiers, and add character to your property. We work with a variety of materials, including timber and concrete sleepers, to suit your style and budget.

View Retaining Walls

Excavation & Earthworks

The foundation of every great landscape is proper site preparation. We provide precision excavation, site cuts, and earthmoving services to create the perfect canvas for your project, ensuring correct drainage and a perfectly level base for structures and lawns.

View Excavation

Turfing & Lawn Installation

A beautiful lawn can completely transform your outdoor space. We supply and install premium turf varieties perfectly suited to the Peninsula climate. Our professional installation ensures a healthy, uniform lawn that's ready to thrive.
