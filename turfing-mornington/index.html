Our 3-Stage Process for a Perfect Lawn

A great lawn is all in the preparation. We follow a proven method for lasting results.

Step 1: Excavation & Levelling

The success of a new lawn starts with a solid foundation. We begin by thoroughly clearing the site of old grass and weeds. Our team then expertly excavates and grades the area, ensuring a perfectly smooth surface with the correct fall to promote healthy drainage.

  • Removal of all existing lawn and weeds
  • Precise grading to ensure proper drainage
  • Laser-levelling for a uniform finish

Step 2: Topsoil & Fertiliser

With the site perfectly graded, we introduce a generous layer of premium, locally sourced topsoil. This nutrient-rich base is essential for strong root development. We also apply a specialised starter fertiliser to give your new lawn all it needs to thrive from day one.

  • Application of nutrient-rich topsoil
  • Inclusion of specialised starter fertiliser
  • Creation of an ideal bed for root growth

Step 3: Turf Installation & Watering

The final step is to lay the fresh, high-quality turf. Our team carefully places each piece, ensuring tight seams for a seamless appearance and trim all edges for a perfect fit against garden beds and pathways. Once laid, the lawn is then given its first deep watering, which settles the turf and promotes the strong root-to-soil contact needed for healthy growth.

  • Expert laying of freshly harvested turf
  • Rolling for optimal root-to-soil contact
  • Initial deep watering and care instructions
