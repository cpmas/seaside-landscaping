Retaining Walls: Engineered for Durability

Retaining walls are essential for managing slopes, preventing erosion, and creating usable, level spaces. We design and construct robust retaining solutions that stand the test of time, with a focus on proper drainage and structural integrity.

  • Concrete Sleeper Walls: Strong, long-lasting, and a popular choice for their modern aesthetic.
  • Timber Retaining Walls: Natural look, ideal for garden beds and more informal settings.
  • Engineered Solutions: Custom designs for complex landscapes and significant earth retention.
  • Integrated Drainage Systems: Essential for preventing water build-up and ensuring longevity.
  • Geofabric & Backfill: Proper materials to support structure and facilitate drainage.
