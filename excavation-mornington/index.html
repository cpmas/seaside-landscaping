Excavation & Earthworks

Precision site preparation for any project.

The Foundation of a Great Landscape

Proper excavation is the critical first step for any successful landscaping or construction project. At Seaside Landscaping, we provide professional earthmoving services across the Mornington Peninsula, ensuring your project starts on a solid, level, and perfectly prepared base.

From tight-access jobs to larger site cuts, our experienced operators use modern machinery to deliver precise results, minimising disruption to your property and preparing your site for the next stage.

Our Excavation Capabilities

We handle a wide range of small-to-medium earthmoving tasks with precision and care.

Site Cuts & Levelling

We create perfectly level and stable platforms for sheds, driveways, turf installation, and entertainment areas. Using laser-level technology, we ensure your base is level and graded accurately, preventing future issues with drainage or stability.

  • Shed & Water Tank Pads
  • Driveway & Pathway Preparation
  • Soil Removal & Spreading

Preparation for Walls & Turf

The success of a new retaining wall or lawn depends entirely on the work done in preperation. We specialise in excavating and preparing sites to ensure a perfect foundation for these key landscaping features, guaranteeing stability for walls and promoting healthy, lush growth for turf.

  • Solid footings for retaining walls
  • Site clearing for new lawn areas
  • Soil removal & turf underlay prep
